Braids on Wheels (BOW) is the Greater Toronto Area’s first on-demand braiding concierge. Elite African braiders, luxury service and at-home convenience — all styles starting at $400. Yes, including the braiding hair!

At BOW, we understand that beauty is personal. That’s why we offer a range of premium styles to suit every individual, from classic braids to the latest trending looks.

Experience the ultimate in hair luxury without stepping foot outside your door.
